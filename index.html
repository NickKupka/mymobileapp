And we Fall Biography

And we fall is a hardcore band from Frankfurt am Main / Hessen / Germany. Consisting of vocalist Phil Ambas, vocalist and
guitarist Nick EBDT, drummer RawMan, bassist Manu aKa Breekatchu and guitarist Tommy Darko, the band has traveled throughout Germany with a unique and award-winning live stage show. Within a short
time the band gave respect and a name in the German hardcore scene.
Following the release of the first EP "Casino Break Down" in early 2013, the management and booking agency "FDM Music" was aware of the band and took them under contract. The confirmation of the
success of the band recorded not only appeared in major German cities again but also a passion for creating and developing the sounds. Thus, continuous work on other songs and plates is a must
for the band. Clothing Technically, the guys move in their specially Developed Style "YoloCore". Flashy and extravagant mix which interests the current youth period with a base of hardcore.
During the "North and Beyond Tour" of 2013, the fan base grew tremendously and rightly reflected the achievements of the band.
And we fall is a hardcore band designed for live gigs and being close to the audience.
